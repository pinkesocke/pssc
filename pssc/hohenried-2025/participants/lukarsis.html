Made up of Luka Radzevičius (vocals), Luka Brasas (guitar), Fiez Kandratavičiūtė (bass guitar) and Fiez Andriulis (drums), the band debuted with Vasara Galvoj Minoras, a track that has gone on to become their signature song.

Blending elements of alternative rock, post-punk and indie, Lukarsis are known for exploring themes of pain, longing and personal liberation in their music; songs which come to life via the group's powerful performances.

2024 saw the release of their first EP, Dausos, which allowed Katarsis to further push the artistic boundaries their fans love to hear them explore.
