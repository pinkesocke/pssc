Not only a musician, he is an accomplished visual artist, consuming and repackaging global trends through his own gaze and sense of humour.

After rising to the top of the Estonian entertainment scene, Timmy Cash's success has quickly spread internationally, too. He has toured the US & Europe and performed at some of the biggest festivals, such as Glastonbury, Sziget and Roskilde.

Timmy's artistic endeavors have been well received in some major publications of fashion, music and film: Dazed has praised his bold manner of bringing attention to today's sensitive topics; Vogue has characterised his sense of style as both rebellious and weirdly funny; while VICE has remarked upon "an extreme uniqueness through his art as a multi-talented, fearless and creative young Eastern European lad".
