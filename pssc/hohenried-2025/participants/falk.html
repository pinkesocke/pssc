The trio quickly earned a reputation for their witty, nostalgic songs celebrating Finnish and Swedish culture; infused with Nordic folk melodies, modern pop and theatrical performances.

The group is made up of Falk Holmström, Falk Åhman and Falk Norrgård. They have released 7 albums, written and performed two musicals at Wasa Theatre in Finland, and even got to put on a huge arena show for their 10th anniversary celebration.

FALK are the first Finnish act to represent Sweden at the Pinke Socke Song Contest, and Bara Bada Bastu is the first Swedish-language song that Sweden has sent to the Contest.
