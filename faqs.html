What is the Pinke Song Contest

The Pinke Socke Song Contest Reference Group is the governing board for all PSBU Members, its purpose being to guide the Pinke Socke Song Contest.

Participation in the Pinke Socke Song Contest is limited to Members of the PSBU or specially invited Associates.

(Associates of the PSBU may be eligible to enter the Pinke Socke Song Contest, this is decided by the Reference Group, the governing body of the Pinke Socke Song Contest, on a case by case basis.)

How is the Pinke Socke Song Contest financed?

- A heavy investment by the PSBU

- Sponsorship agreements

- Donations

So if you enjoy the PSSC and want to support the Contest, please consider becoming a sponsor or making a donation!

The Pinke Socke Song Contest is a long running non-political, entertainment event which is designed to bring audiences together.

The original idea in 2022 was to offer all Members of the PSBU, the world's leading alliance of sock-puppet media, an exciting yearly entertainment television show to take part in and to test the limits of live television broadcast technology.

The Pinke Socke Song Contest's values are of universality and inclusivity and our proud tradition of celebrating diversity through music which is reflected in our global audiences.

How to Watch

You can watch the Grand Finale of the Pinke Socke Song Contest live at the Bosse Theatre.

Public and media enquiries, questions and suggestions can be sent to the organisers.
